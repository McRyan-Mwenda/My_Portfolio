My journey

Throughout my life, I thought my path was already drawn out. But life has an interesting way of taking you to places you have never thought of going, paths you never thought you would walk, passions discovered through mere experimentations. It is through these unexpected ventures that I realized I had a passion for 3D animations after being introduced to a Autodesk Maya tutorial by a friend.

Given my new found passion, the next logical step for me was to join university and pursue a course which would allow me to learn more about 3D animation as a hobby while pursuing a course in Information Technology. I joined the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology and later on, Mount Kenya University; while I was learning, I found a passion in 3D animation and I am in the process of making it my career.

While it has been quite exhausting journey, I have learnt so much and I am becoming more confident in my skills. I have also been fortunate enough to get help and mentorship from a very good counsellor. With her assistance I rediscovered my passion for 3D artsistry and I am able to develop clarity of where I am at the moment and where I wanted to be in future.

One of the things that has become apparent to me throughout this journey, is that if you have a dream and make achieving your dream your top priority, nothing will stand in your way.
